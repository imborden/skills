# Aesthetic Direction Matrix — Greenfield / Zero-Brand Work

Use this only when there is **no** attached brand or design system. Select **exactly one** lane, declare it out loud, and lean entirely into its extreme characteristics. **Never mix or blend lanes** — combining them produces muddy, generic AI defaults.

When a lane's identity deliberately uses a technique the base skill's anti-slop bans warn about (gradients, glow, rounded shapes), executing it *consistently within that lane* is correct. The bans target the unconsidered default; the chosen lane governs.

**Type specimens** below are concrete, free on Google Fonts, and chosen to fit the lane — use them as defaults unless you have a better lane-true choice. None are the banned faces (Inter / Roboto / Arial / Helvetica / Fraunces).

---

## Editorial / Magazine
- **Vibe:** High-end print layout — cultural journals, fashion houses, premium long-form journalism.
- **Typography:** High-contrast serif display paired with a sharp, geometric sans body.
- **Type:** Display — Bodoni Moda, Playfair Display, or Cormorant Garamond. Body — DM Sans or Archivo.
- **Composition:** Oversized headers, generous negative space, strict asymmetric grids.

## Industrial / Technical
- **Vibe:** Blueprints, command-line interfaces, shipping manifests, raw manufacturing data.
- **Typography:** Pure monospace or high-density grotesque faces.
- **Type:** JetBrains Mono, IBM Plex Mono, or Space Mono; for dense grotesque, Archivo Narrow or Oswald.
- **Composition:** Hard borders, high information density, raw layouts, a total ban on `border-radius`.

## Luxury / Refined
- **Vibe:** Elite horology, fine-art galleries, bespoke tailoring, quiet luxury.
- **Typography:** Elegant, tracked-out lapidary serifs or ultra-light sans.
- **Type:** Cormorant, EB Garamond, or Cinzel (lapidary caps); ultra-light sans — Jost or Marcellus.
- **Composition:** Massive padding, microscopic details, muted monochromatic palettes.

## Retro-Futuristic
- **Vibe:** Cold War aerospace tech, classic sci-fi anime, mainframe terminal concepts.
- **Typography:** Highly stylized display geometry.
- **Type:** Orbitron, Michroma, Chakra Petch, or Rajdhani.
- **Composition:** High contrast, sharp neon-on-dark accents, kinetic structural lines.

## Swiss International / Functionalist
- **Vibe:** Objective, rational, devoid of decorative trends — mid-century museum archives, modernist transit design systems.
- **Typography:** Strict, heavy, un-rounded neo-grotesque sans-serifs. Massive display type contrasting with tiny functional metadata text.
- **Type:** Archivo (Black), Hanken Grotesk, Familjen Grotesk, or Barlow; metadata in IBM Plex Mono.
- **Composition:** Driven by a rigorous, visible mathematical grid. Extreme left-alignment, zero centered text, thin solid dividing lines slicing the canvas.
- **Color:** Flat fields of **cool or neutral** white and gray (reserve warm cream for the Organic lane), with a single aggressive non-gradient primary spot color (Swiss red, safety orange, or cobalt blue).

## Neo-Brutalist / Web3 Raw
- **Vibe:** High-contrast, intentionally unpolished, intensely digital. Treats the browser like a raw OS, not a glossy app.
- **Typography:** Clunky, heavy geometric sans-serifs mixed with harsh monospace for accents and labels.
- **Type:** Space Grotesk, Syne, or Archivo; monospace accents in Space Mono.
- **Composition:** Heavy thick black outer strokes on every container, card, and button. Zero `border-radius`. Hard, un-blurred black drop-shadows shifted exactly 4px down and right for a fake 3D pop.
- **Color:** High-saturation, high-contrast pairings (toxic neon green, bright lemon yellow, or electric cyan backgrounds clashing against stark black text).

## Cyber-Y2K / High-Gloss Tech
- **Vibe:** Early-2000s futuristic optimism — original PlayStation menus, translucent plastic hardware, early liquid UI.
- **Typography:** Wide, extended, low-height futuristic sans-serifs, paired with clean highly-readable micro-sans for body.
- **Type:** Saira (or Saira Condensed), Exo 2, or Michroma for display; IBM Plex Sans for body.
- **Composition:** Multi-layered layouts with heavy blurred glassmorphism, glowing outer strokes, translucent overlays, and crosshair/target grid indicators in section corners.
- **Color:** Deep dark metallic grays or midnight blues as canvas, punctuated by hyper-vibrant glowing accents (electric purple, neon lime, radioactive aqua).

## Organic / Wabi-Sabi
- **Vibe:** Earthy, tactile, human, intentionally imperfect — pottery studios, premium wellness journals, slow-living editorial.
- **Typography:** Warm, soft, or irregular serifs with generous tracking for headers, paired with a legible friendly humanist sans for body.
- **Type:** Spectral, Newsreader, or Lora for headers; Mulish or Work Sans for body.
- **Composition:** Fluid, loose, off-center layouts. Massive padding and asymmetric negative space. Avoids hard boxes — separates content through spatial grouping or soft organic dividers.
- **Color:** Low-contrast, muted, warm earth tones (rich oatmeal, terracotta, olive green, charcoal, warm sand) — never pure black or blinding white.

## Vaporwave / Glitch-Art
- **Vibe:** Surreal, melancholic — 80s/90s mall culture mixed with early-internet computer graphics and lo-fi distortion.
- **Typography:** Bold italicized 80s display faces or retro-serifs, mixed with stark pixel/bitmap fonts for navigation and secondary UI tags.
- **Type:** Display — Monoton, Bungee, or Audiowide; pixel/bitmap — VT323, Press Start 2P, or Silkscreen.
- **Composition:** Overlapping windows, marquee-style tickers, scanning lines, subtle CSS glitch/static effects on text hover.
- **Color:** Dreamy pastel-to-neon — soft pinks, deep purples, teals, saturated magentas against stark white or deep violet.

## Playful / Toy-Like
- **Vibe:** Whimsical, tactile, energetic — an interactive playground or physical board game, not sterile software.
- **Typography:** Chunky, geometric, hyper-rounded sans-serif display fonts paired with clean friendly rounded geometric faces for UI body.
- **Type:** Fredoka, Baloo 2, Quicksand, or Nunito.
- **Composition:** Soft over-inflated pill shapes, oversized buttons that visibly "sink" on click, thick playful borders, asymmetrical bouncy layout clusters.
- **Color:** Vibrant cheerful primary/secondary blocks (sun-yellow, rich cream, sky blue, soft tomato red) with dark navy or charcoal text instead of harsh black.
